## How to Use

### Calling Your Pet:
1. Press ESC ‚Üí Animals tab
2. Look for the whistle button (bottom-right, has a horn icon)
3. Click on any pet in the list
4. Pet switches to follow mode and comes to you
5. Only works if pet is in your current location

### Tracking Pets:
- **Off-screen**: Arrows with pet icons show at screen edges
- **With NPC Map Locations**: Pets appear on the map with their names

## Known Limitations
- Whistle only works in same location (can't teleport across maps)
- NPC Map Locations integration is automatic (no configuration needed)
