# Stardew Modding

This is my mod repo (CodeKokeshi). It’s where I make my mods and stuff.

## What’s in here

### Officially Finished
- **BuyAnimalsFullyMatured**: Purchased barn/coop animals are instantly fully matured and ready to produce.
- **BypassAllDoors**: Bypass door restrictions (time, friendship, festivals, etc.) with toggles.
- **InstantFishBite**: Makes fish bite instantly.
- **MaxQuality**: Manually apply iridium quality to your inventory and open chests via hotkey (no automatic changes).
- **NoStamina**: Removes the stamina mechanic.

### Done Not Uploaded
- **CombatCheats**: One-hit kill, infinite HP, max drop rates, and 100% crit chance (all optional).
- **CustomAnimalHearts**: Set custom heart values for pets and farm animals.
- **InstantBuildings**: Buildings, house upgrades, and community upgrades complete instantly.
- **InstantUpgradeTools**: Instantly upgrades tools without waiting days.

### Updating
- **BountifulForaging**: Boosts forage, artifact spots, and beach items with configurable spawns.
- **FixMuseumInventory**: Fixes museum inventory UI issues and adds a move button.
- **WorkingPets**: Pets help with debris, trees, boulders, scavenging, map tracking, and more.
