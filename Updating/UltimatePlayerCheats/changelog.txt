v1.0.0

Master Toggle
- Mod Enabled: Enable or disable all cheats
- Open Menu Hotkey: Press this key to open this config menu directly (default: K)

Movement & Speed
- Speed Multiplier: Movement speed multiplier (1.0 = normal, 2.0 = 2x speed, 10.0 = 10x speed)
- Added Speed Bonus: Flat speed bonus added to base movement
- No Clip: Walk through walls, buildings, and all obstacles
- Always Run: Always running, never walking

Health & Stamina
- Infinite Stamina: Never get tired, stamina always stays at max
- Infinite Health (Invincibility): Never die, health always stays at max, complete invincibility
- Max Stamina Override: Override max stamina (0 = use default)
- Max Health Override: Override max health (0 = use default)
- Stamina Regen Per Second: How much stamina regenerates every second (0 = none)
- Health Regen Per Second: How much health regenerates every second (0 = none)

Combat
- One Hit Kill: All enemies die in one hit
- 100% Critical Chance: All attacks are critical hits
- Damage Multiplier: Multiply all weapon damage (1.0 = normal, 5.0 = 5x damage)
- Crit Damage Multiplier: Multiply critical hit damage (1.0 = normal)
- Added Attack: Flat bonus attack points
- Added Defense: Flat bonus defense points
- Added Immunity: Flat bonus immunity points
- Invincibility Duration (ms): How long you're invincible after getting hit (default 1200ms)
- No Monster Spawns: Removes all monsters from the current location

Tools & Farming
- Tool Area Multiplier: Multiply the area affected by hoe/watering can (1 = normal, 3 = 3x3, 5 = 5x5)
- Tool Power Override: Force tool charge level (-1 = normal, 0-5 = power level)
- No Tool Stamina Cost: Using tools doesn't cost stamina
- Infinite Water: Watering can never runs out
- Axe Power Bonus: Additional power for axe, makes chopping easier
- Pickaxe Power Bonus: Additional power for pickaxe, makes mining easier

Items & Inventory
- Magnetic Radius Multiplier: Item pickup range multiplier (1.0 = normal, 5.0 = 5x range)
- Added Magnetic Radius: Flat bonus to pickup range in pixels (128 = 2 tiles)
- Infinite Items: Items don't get consumed when used

Skills & Levels
- XP Multiplier: Multiply all XP gains (1.0 = normal, 100 = 100x XP, 1000 = instant level ups)
- Farming Level Override: Force farming level (-1 = normal)
- Mining Level Override: Force mining level (-1 = normal)
- Foraging Level Override: Force foraging level (-1 = normal)
- Fishing Level Override: Force fishing level (-1 = normal)
- Combat Level Override: Force combat level (-1 = normal)

Luck
- Always Max Luck: Every day is the luckiest day possible, applies immediately
- Daily Luck Override: Set exact daily luck (-1.0 = disabled, range -0.1 to 0.12), applies immediately

Fishing
- Instant Fish Bite: Fish bite immediately when you cast
- Instant Catch: Skip the fishing minigame entirely
- Max Fish Quality: All caught fish are iridium quality
- Always Find Treasure: Always find treasure when fishing

Quality & Prices
- Force Harvest Quality: Force quality of all harvested crops (-1=disabled, 0=normal, 1=silver, 2=gold, 4=iridium)
- Force Forage Quality: Force quality of all foraged items (-1=disabled, 0=normal, 1=silver, 2=gold, 4=iridium), only affects forageables that naturally have quality
- Sell Price Multiplier: Multiply prices when selling (1.0 = normal, 2.0 = 2x profit)
- Buy Price Multiplier: Multiply prices when buying (1.0 = normal, 0.5 = half price, 0 = free)

Relationships
- Friendship Multiplier: Multiply friendship gains (1.0 = normal, 10.0 = 10x faster)
- No Friendship Decay: Friendship never decreases

Time
- Freeze Time: Time never passes
- Freeze Time Indoors: Time stops when you're inside buildings
- Never Pass Out: Stay awake past 2am without passing out

Miscellaneous
- Max Animal Happiness: All farm animals are always at max happiness and friendship
- Crops Never Die: Crops survive season changes and lack of water
- Instant Crop Growth: All crops across all locations grow to full harvest in real-time
- Instant Tree Growth: All regular trees (oak, maple, pine, etc.) grow to full size in real-time
- Instant Fruit Tree Growth: All fruit trees grow to full maturity in real-time
