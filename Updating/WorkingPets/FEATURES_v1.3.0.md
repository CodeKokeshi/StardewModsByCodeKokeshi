## How to Use

### Foraging While Following:
1. Talk to your pet and select "Follow me"
2. Walk around any location with forageable items
3. Your pet will automatically approach and collect nearby items
4. Check their inventory periodically to claim your loot!

### Calling Your Pet:
1. Press ESC ‚Üí Animals tab
2. Look for the whistle button (bottom-right, has a horn icon)
3. Click on any pet in the list
4. Pet switches to follow mode and comes to you
5. Only works if pet is in your current location

### Tracking Pets:
- **Off-screen**: Arrows with pet icons show at screen edges
- **With NPC Map Locations**: Pets appear on the map with their names

## Configuration Options

New in 1.3.0:
- **Forage While Following**: Enable/disable automatic forage collection (default: enabled)
- **Follow Outside Farm**: Allow pets to follow in non-farm areas (default: disabled)

## Known Limitations
- Whistle only works in same location (can't call across maps)
- NPC Map Locations integration is automatic (no configuration needed)
- Foraging only works while in follow mode
