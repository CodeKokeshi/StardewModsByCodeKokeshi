v1.0.0

Master Toggle
- Mod Enabled: Enable or disable all world cheats
- Open Menu Hotkey: Press this key to open this config menu directly (default: L)

Buildings & Construction
- Instant Building Construction: New buildings finish constructing immediately when placed, Robin goes home right away (skips on festival days and Green Rain year 1)
- Instant Building Upgrades: Building upgrades (e.g., barn to big barn) complete instantly instead of waiting days
- Instant House Upgrades: Farmhouse upgrades from Robin complete instantly, your house expands immediately after purchase
- Instant Community Upgrades: Community upgrades like Pam's house and town shortcuts complete instantly
- Free Building Construction: Buildings cost no gold or materials to construct, works with Robin and the Wizard

Tool Upgrades
- Instant Tool Upgrade: Tool upgrades at Clint's blacksmith complete instantly, your upgraded tool is ready the same day you drop it off

Machines & Processing
- Instant Machine Processing: All machines (kegs, preserves jars, furnaces, cheese press, etc.) produce output instantly with zero processing time
- Free Geode Processing: Processing geodes at Clint's blacksmith is free (normally costs 25g each)

Shopping & Economy
- Free Shop Purchases: All shop purchases cost nothing, works with Pierre, Robin, Clint, Marnie, Willy, Sandy, Krobus, the Traveling Cart, and all other shops
- Free Crafting: Crafting recipes do not consume ingredients, you can craft anything even without the materials

Weather Control
- Tomorrow's Weather: Override tomorrow's weather forecast (Sun, Rain, Storm, Snow, Wind), set to No Override to let the game decide normally
