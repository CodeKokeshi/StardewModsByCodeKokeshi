BOUNTIFUL FORAGING - CHANGELOG

Version 2.1.0 - January 23, 2026
--------------------------------
BEACH ITEMS FIX - Coral & Sea Urchins now boosted!

ISSUE: Beach-specific items (coral, nautilus shell, sea urchins) were NOT being
boosted because vanilla uses HARDCODED spawning in Beach.DayUpdate() that is 
completely separate from the SpawnForageData system.

NEW BEACH ITEMS SETTINGS:
- Boost Beach Items: Toggle extra coral/urchin spawning
- Extra Coral Spawns: Spawns coral and nautilus shells in tidepool area (default: 10)
- Extra Sea Urchin Spawns: Spawns sea urchins on shore strip (default: 5)

TECHNICAL DETAILS:
Vanilla Beach.DayUpdate() spawns:
- Coral/Nautilus Shell: Rectangle(65,11,25,12) - 80% coral, 20% nautilus
- Sea Urchin: Rectangle(66,24,19,1) - EXTREMELY rare (0.25 * 0.1 chance!)

Version 2.0.0 - January 23, 2026
--------------------------------
COMPLETE REWRITE - VANILLA-ALIGNED SYSTEM

This version is a complete rewrite with a cleaner, simpler approach.
The mod now directly uses vanilla spawning logic with configurable boosts.

 FORAGE SETTINGS:
- Min Daily Forage: Minimum spawn attempts per day (vanilla: 1-2)
- Max Daily Forage: Maximum spawn attempts per day (vanilla: 4-6)  
- Max Forage At Once: Cap on forage items per location (vanilla: 6)
- Forage Spawn Chance %: Multiplier for individual item chance (100% = vanilla)

 ARTIFACT SPOT (DIGGING SPOT) SETTINGS:
- Boost Artifact Spots: Toggle extra spawning on/off
- Extra Spawn Attempts: Number of extra artifact spot attempts per day
- Seed Spot Chance %: Chance for 4-leaf clover instead of artifact spot (vanilla: ~17%)

HOW IT WORKS:
- Uses EXACT vanilla spawning logic (copied from decompiled GameLocation.spawnObjects)
- Forage spawns on "Spawnable" tiles (vanilla behavior)
- Artifact spots spawn on "Diggable" tiles (vanilla behavior)
- Supports modded forageables that use vanilla's SpawnForageData system
- Runs AFTER vanilla spawning to add extra items

DEFAULTS (boosted but balanced):
- Min Daily Forage: 10 (vanilla ~1-2)
- Max Daily Forage: 25 (vanilla ~4-6)
- Max Forage At Once: 50 (vanilla 6)
- Forage Chance: 150% (50% boost)
- Extra Artifact Spots: 15
- Seed Spot Chance: 17% (vanilla)
- Extra Coral: 10
- Extra Sea Urchins: 5
